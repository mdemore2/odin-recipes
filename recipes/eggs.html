Eggs

Description

A soft-boiled egg is an egg that has been boiled for a short period of time, so that the white is set but the yolk is still soft and runny. The texture of the white should be firm but not rubbery, and the yolk should be slightly runny and velvety. The ideal cooking time for a soft-boiled egg will vary depending on the size of the egg, but it is typically around 5-7 minutes.

Soft-boiled eggs can be eaten plain or topped with salt, pepper, or your favorite seasonings. They can also be used in a variety of dishes, such as salads, sandwiches, and sushi.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with cold water and bring it to a boil over high heat.
  2. Gently lower the egg(s) into the boiling water using a slotted spoon.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 6 minutes for a runny yolk, or 7 minutes for a slightly more set yolk.
  4. Immediately remove the eggs from the water and plunge them into a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process.
  5. Peel the eggs and enjoy!
